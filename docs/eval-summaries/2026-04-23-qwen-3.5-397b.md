## Augmentation Trend (L0-L4)

Augmentation levels L1-L4 apply progressive AST transforms to source code before translation.

| Level | Pass Rate | 95% CI | N |
|-------|-----------|--------|---|
| L1 | 72.5% | [59.1%, 82.9%] | 51 |
| L2 | 66.7% | [53.0%, 78.0%] | 51 |
| L3 | 66.7% | [53.0%, 78.0%] | 51 |
| L4 | 66.7% | [53.0%, 78.0%] | 51 |

**Cochran-Armitage trend test:** z = -0.605, p = 0.545 (not significant)

The L1→L4 decline is small and not statistically significant. Augmentation transforms appear cosmetic — they do not meaningfully degrade translation quality. Note: L0 comparison requires the pass@k campaign data (pass@1 mean 23.7%), which is not directly comparable due to different temperature settings (0.7 vs 0.0).
