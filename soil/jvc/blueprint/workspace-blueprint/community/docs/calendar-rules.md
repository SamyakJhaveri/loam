# Content Calendar Rules

<!--
TEACHING NOTE: Cadence and scheduling guidelines.
This doc prevents the "post everything at once" trap.
-->

## Publishing Cadence

| Format | Frequency | Day |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Newsletter | Weekly | Tuesday morning |
| Blog post | 1-2 per week | Monday or Thursday |
| Twitter/X | Daily | — |
| LinkedIn | 2-3 per week | Tues/Wed/Thurs |

## Content Mix

- 60% educational (tutorials, explainers, how-tos)
- 25% product-related (launches, updates, demos)
- 15% community (events, highlights, conversations)

## Coordination Rules

- New feature launches: blog post first, then social + newsletter to amplify
- Event promotion: start 2 weeks before, 3 posts across platforms
- Don't publish competing content on the same day (e.g., two blog posts)
