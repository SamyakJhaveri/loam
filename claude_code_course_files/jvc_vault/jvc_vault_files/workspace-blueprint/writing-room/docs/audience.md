## Primary: Working Developers

- 2-8 years experience
- Use our product (or evaluating it)
- Want practical solutions, not thought leadership
- Already know the basics — don't over-explain fundamentals
- Read on phones during commutes, scan headers first

## Secondary: Technical Decision Makers

- Team leads, architects, CTOs at small-mid companies
- Care about: reliability, cost, migration path, team adoption
- Want evidence (benchmarks, case studies, architecture diagrams)
- Less code, more strategy

## Tertiary: Developer Advocates & Community

- Other DevRel people, open source contributors
- Want to share our content with their audiences
- Care about accuracy and fairness to alternatives
