Critical jump at L2 → L3: from 1 transform to 3, and from 33% to 66% of sites. Transforms run sequentially — each one rewrites the code, then the next operates on the already-modified source. This compounding triggers edge cases that don't appear at L1/L2.

Already Fixed (cherry-picked from erel/aug — 2026-03-19)
