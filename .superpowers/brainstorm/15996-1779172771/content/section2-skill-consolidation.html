Section 2: Skill Consolidation

24 core skills → 13 callable + AGENTS.md persistent context

The Principle

Vercel's research found that persistent context (AGENTS.md) achieves 100% task success vs 79% for skills. The insight: guidance that's always available beats guidance that must be discovered and loaded on demand.

Rule of thumb: If a skill is mostly guidance (rules, patterns, checklists), fold it into AGENTS.md. If it's a procedure (multi-step execution with state), keep it as a callable skill.
